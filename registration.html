Regular Registration takes place in July and August prior to the beginning of the school year. Students may register early in May. You may register in person during one of our scheduled drop-in registration dates.

Early Registration

Our early registration starts on May 25- June 30. For one student it is $40.00. If you have more than on student you pay an additional $5.00 per student. For example, if you are enrolling three students it would be $50.00.

Standard Registration

Standard registraiton begins on July 1- August 14. For one student it is $55.00. If you have more than on student you pay an additional $5.00 per student.

Late Registration

Anything after August 14 is considered late registration. If you are enrolling one student it is $65.00. If you have more than on student you pay an additional $5.00 per student.
